How to apply for these courses in search results?

You can click the Course name or college name to get detailed information on it.
Click the "Add to my selection" button next to the courses you would like to apply for - they will appear in your "My selection" box
When you're finished, simply click on "Apply now" to continue with your application.
Note: You can fine tune this search result course list again by using advanced search , sort by or by modifying the search criteria according to your choice.
