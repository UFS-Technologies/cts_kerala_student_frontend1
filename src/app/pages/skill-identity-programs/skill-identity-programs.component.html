Skill Identity Programs

 

At Council for Technology & Science Kerala (C-TECH), we understand that not all skills come from classrooms. Thousands of skilled individuals in India and abroad have developed expertise through hands-on experience but lack formal certification. To bridge this gap, C-TECH proudly introduces the Skill Identity Programs—a national-level initiative to validate, certify, and empower skilled workers.

What is a Skill Identity Program?

The Skill Identity Program is a structured initiative designed to:

Recognize the practical skills of individuals who gained experience through employment, self-learning, or informal training.

Provide official certification based on skill tests and trade-specific evaluations.

Certificates are issued after practical skill assessments, trade knowledge verification, and document validation, ensuring that the certification reflects true competency.

Who is it for?

Technicians, workers, and freelancers with 2+ years of experience in a trade

Individuals without formal academic qualifications

Migrant workers, returnees from abroad, and informal sector workers

Anyone seeking recognition for prior learning (RPL)

Global Relevance & Demand

In today’s global economy, skilled manpower is in high demand across sectors such as construction, hospitality, healthcare, logistics, IT, and more. Many countries, especially in the Middle East, Europe, and Southeast Asia, now prefer or require proof of skill proficiency when hiring workers—even more than academic degrees.

The Skill Identity Certificate from C-TECH:

Helps job seekers prove their capabilities abroad

Supports visa and recruitment processes by adding credibility

Aligns with international skill standards and frameworks like NSQF, NOSS (Malaysia), and NVQs (UK)

Key Advantages

Enhances Employability in Global & Domestic Job Markets

Verification via QR Code / Digital ID

Supports Migration and International Job Applications

Recognized for RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning)

Official Certification of Experience-Based Skills

Trades Covered (Examples):

Electrical Works, Fitter, Plumbing & Sanitation, Welding & Fabrication, Mobile Phone Repair, Carpentry & Masonry, Beauty Therapy, Office Assistance, CCTV & Security Systems …and more
