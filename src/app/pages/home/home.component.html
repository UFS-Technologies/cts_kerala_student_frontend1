Certificate & Diploma Programs

Council for Technology & Science Kerala (C-TECH), operating under Kairali Sevak Samaj, conducts a wide range of Diploma and Skill Training Programs across Kerala. These programs are delivered in collaboration with multiple authorized training centres that are associated with C-TECH and follow our curriculum, quality standards, and assessment protocols.

Skill Proficiency Programs


The Skill Proficiency Program by C-TECH is designed to certify practical skills in a wide range of trades and technical areas. These programs are especially beneficial for those who already have experience or informal training in a particular skill, but lack formal certification.Through a structured assessment and certification process,C-TECH validates your abilities

Authorised Training Centers

C-TECH ensures a standardized and transparent examination process across all authorised training centers. Each center is responsible for conducting examinations in compliance with C-TECH’s prescribed guidelines.

Examinations can be conducted in both online and offline modes, as per the course requirements and student convenience. The schedule and format will be determined according to the course duration and syllabus.

Follow C-TECH’s examination schedule without deviation

Provide necessary infrastructure for online and offline exams.

Maintain strict confidentiality and integrity in question paper handling

Upload exam results and reports promptly through the center login portal.
