About Us

Council for Technology & Science Kerala (C-TECH) is a Skill Training Institution under the Kairali Sevak Samaj (KSS). C-TECH's goal is to help students discover their talents and find the right career. For this, C-TECH is conducting various job trainings in collaboration with Skill Development Institutions in Kerala. After the training, a skill test is conducted and a certificate is issued. Through C-TECH Certificate/Diploma students find employment in the private sector.

C-tech has been doing excellent work in the field of skill development for the last 20 years. Thousands of students are working in different countries of the world through C-TECH Certification. C-TECH's Microsoft Office Diploma (MS Office) has been approved by the Public Service Commission of Kerala (PSC). C-TECH Certificate/Diploma can be registered in all employment exchanges in Kerala.

Activities

  • Create an end-to-end implementation framework for skill development, which provides opportunities for life-long learning. This includes: incorporation of skilling in the school curriculum, providing opportunities for quality long and short-term skill training, by providing gainful employment and ensuring career progression that meets the aspirations of trainees.
  • Align employer/industry demand and workforce productivity with trainees’ aspirations for sustainable livelihoods, by creating a framework for outcome focused training.
  • Establish and enforce cross-sectoral, nationally and internationally acceptable standards for skill training in the country by creating a sound quality assurance framework for skilling, applicable to all Ministries, States and private training providers.

Need help searching for courses and programmes?

You can find courses and programmes that interest you by following several easy steps:
01 - Enter a word or words in the box and click "Search"
02 - Look through your search results and click on "More information" for, you guessed it, more information about each course and programme!
03 - Click the "Add" button next to the courses you would like to apply for - they will appear in your "My selection" box
04 - When you're finished, simply click on "To application" to continue with your application. Cant find what you are looking for? You can search by entering parts of a word or a whole word - you will see a list of suggestions appear that might be what you're looking for.(But selecting that particular suggestion will result in that course details only). You can also combine
several search words, or use the option of department (stream / subject) search alone for more & wide number of options
Advanced search
is useful if you would like a list of results that are more specific and fine-tuned to what you'd like to study, and how you'd like to study. You can choose to see only courses and programmes that fit certain criteria, like level and instructional time, or choose to only search for courses and programmes that are currently open for application. Begin by entering a search word or words in the green box. Then click on "Advanced Search" and select the different items that are important to you. When you click "Search", the results list will match the criteria you have specified. You can also limit your search to specific universities or specific durations, Intakes, IELTS requirements & internship availabilities by using this optional advanced search option.
