Document Verification – Guidelines

Council for Technology & Science Kerala (C-TECH) provides a document verification facility for Government Departments, Companies, Embassies, Verification Agencies, and other Authorized Organizations. Please read the following instructions carefully before submitting a request:

Modes of Verification

Email Verification: Send a scanned copy of the certificate and mark list to info@ctskerala.in along with the payment receipt (if applicable).

Physical Verification: Submit documents directly to our office for processing.

Letter-Based Verification: Official letters requesting verification may be sent to our registered address with all required details.

Note: We do not provide verification through phone calls. All requests must be made in writing (email/letter/physical submission). Processing time depends on the completeness of documents submitted. Processing time: 7–10 working days (may vary depending on case complexity. Only written requests with proper documentation will be entertained. Verification reports will be issued only to the requesting organization, not to third parties without authorization.

Student Result Lookup (Online)

You can check basic result details by entering the student registration number on our website. This online search result is for reference only and is not an official verification document. Official verification requests must be submitted as described above. Due to system limitations, not all student records may be available online. Technical errors or data sync delays may sometimes occur; in such cases, official verification requests must be submitted manually. C-TECH does not upload every student's document online for privacy and security reasons.
