About Us

Established in 1993, the Council for Technology and Science Kerala (CTech) has been actively contributing to the field of education in Kerala for over three decades. Now functioning as a registered Non-Governmental Organization (NGO), CTech operates under the aegis of Kairali Sevak Samaj, with a dedicated focus on skill development and vocational empowerment.

CTech was founded with the vision of creating an inclusive, employment-oriented skill education ecosystem. To achieve this, we have built a strong statewide network of affiliated training institutions across Kerala. Many of these centers, now associated with us as Institutional Members, are among the leading skill training centers in the state, offering industry-relevant, practical learning experiences.

We offer more than 100 specialized diploma and training programs in various sectors such as healthcare, fashion technology, IT, mobile technology, office administration, mechanical services, and more. These courses are delivered through our network of CTech-recognized training centers, enabling students to gain certified, hands-on training that aligns with national standards and global workforce needs.

Thousands of students have benefitted from our training, gaining access to promising job opportunities both in India and abroad. Our programs are designed to cater to the needs of school dropouts, job seekers, working professionals, and aspiring entrepreneurs – making skill education accessible to all segments of society.

We continue to expand through collaborations with Sector Skill Councils, NSDC-aligned bodies, and government-recognized institutions, ensuring credibility and relevance in everything we offer. At CTech, we believe in empowering people with skills that lead to real success.
