How to get a Promotional code?

Please contact your nearest : IELTS / Nodal centre or check the promotional email / message from our promoters or previous students from where you got our reference.

You can also apply without a code, but using a code saves you up to 500 $ on application fees.

ID pages of passport (First & last pages) & all mark sheets up to your highest qualification are only needed for an offer letter

01 - If you already have IELTS -You can upload IELTS score card for a full confirmed offer / admission letter. Or else
02 - Upload basic documents & get the conditional offer letter (showing the condition to clear the required IELTS) without IELTS .Once you get your scores you can later upload it and get a new full confirmed offer letter. For more details you can refer the required documents page of this website.
03 - Conditional offer ( conditions like - need to submit IELTS score card / final year mark sheet in case of mark list waiting students) is enough for starting the education loan processing in case you need one.
04 - Some colleges (very few) may ask for an IELTS test booking proof to issue the conditional offer letter (eg: Conestoga College, Canada).
05 - Anyways Basic documents are only needed for starting the application & obtaining a conditional offer letter . If any documents are missing or pending we will inform you by mail & in the message section of this website so that you can easily upload them later.
06 - Some countries like Singapore, Portugal, Sweden (for P.G in Engineering) do not need IELTS / TOEFL and issue a confirmed offer letter (final acceptance / admission ) with these Basic documents.
07 - Countries with English as the mother tongue generally requires IELTS score.(eg: Canada, U.K, Ireland,New Zealand, Australia). It is not mandatory for other countries.
08 - TOEFL is accepted instead of an IELTS score. PTE (Pearson Test of English) is also accepted.
09 - PTE is accepted in some colleges in Canada (like Fanshawe, Confederation )..
10 - Documents like Work experience certificates, other extra curricular activities etc are optional documents.
