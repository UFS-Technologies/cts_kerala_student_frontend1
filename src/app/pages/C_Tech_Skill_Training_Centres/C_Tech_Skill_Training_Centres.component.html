Examination Guidelines for Authorised Training Centers

C-TECH ensures a standardized and transparent examination process across all authorised training centers. Each center is responsible for conducting examinations in compliance with C-TECH’s prescribed guidelines, ensuring quality and fairness for all learners.

Examinations can be conducted in both online and offline modes, as per the course requirements and student convenience. The schedule and format will be determined according to the course duration and syllabus.

Training centers are required to:

Follow C-TECH’s examination schedule without deviation.

Provide necessary infrastructure for online and offline exams.

Maintain strict confidentiality and integrity in question paper handling.

Upload exam results and reports promptly through the center login portal.

This system ensures uniformity in assessment standards across all centers, helping maintain the credibility and recognition of C-TECH certifications.

Verification Charges

Note: We do not provide verification through phone calls. All requests must be made in writing (email/letter/physical submission). Processing time depends on the completeness of documents submitted. Processing time: 7–10 working days (may vary depending on case complexity. Only written requests with proper documentation will be entertained. Verification reports will be issued only to the requesting organization, not to third parties without authorization.
