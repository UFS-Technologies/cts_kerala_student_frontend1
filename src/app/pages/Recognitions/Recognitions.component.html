Recognitions & Affiliations

The Council for Technology & Science Kerala (C-Tech) was formed under the auspices of Kairali Sevak Samaj (KSS), with a clear mission to promote skill development and employment readiness across Kerala and beyond.

Kairali Sevak Samaj (KSS), established in 1993, is a registered NGO under the Travancore-Cochin Literary, Scientific, and Charitable Societies Registration Act, 1955, Government of Kerala. It is also a registered entity with the NITI Aayog (NGO DARPAN) under the Government of India. Over the past three decades, KSS has earned widespread recognition for its commitment to education, vocational training, and social welfare.

Course & Certificate Approvals

ISO 9001 Accreditation:

Initially certified with ISO 9001:2008 and currently accredited with ISO 9001:2015, ensuring world-class quality in training and assessment.

Kerala Public Service Commission (PSC) Approval:

Select diploma courses conducted by C-Tech are approved for consideration by the Kerala PSC.

Employment Exchange Registration:

C-Tech certificates are valid for registration with the Employment Exchange under the Government of Kerala.

Embassy & Apostille Attestation:

C-Tech certificates are accepted for Embassy Attestation and Apostille Legalization, making them valid for employment and higher studies abroad.

Recognition by Top Multinational Companies:

Major corporations such as Wipro, Infosys, and other MNCs recognize C-Tech certification for employment screening.

Skill Testing & Certification

C-Tech also conducts independent Skill Tests to assess and certify the real-time skills of learners. In today's competitive job market, proven skills and verifiable certification are vital for securing employment. C-Tech ensures both by offering recognized certification backed by rigorous training and practical evaluation.

Council for Technology & Science Kerala (C-Tech) remains committed to building a future-ready generation through recognized, affordable, and impactful skill education — rooted in trust, powered by partnerships, and driven by results.
