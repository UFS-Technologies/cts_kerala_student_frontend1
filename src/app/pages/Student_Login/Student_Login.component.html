Examination System

C-TECH programs maintain a fair, transparent, and learner-friendly examination system designed to evaluate both theoretical knowledge and practical skills. Examinations are conducted in two modes – Online and Offline – to ensure maximum flexibility for students across different locations.

The type and frequency of examinations are determined according to the duration and nature of each course. Short-term programs may have a single final assessment, while long-term diploma courses include periodic evaluations, assignments, and a final examination.

Our examination pattern ensures:

Convenience – Students can choose between online and offline modes.

Practical Orientation – Assessment includes hands-on projects, viva, and practical tests where applicable.

Transparency – Results and feedback are shared promptly to support learning improvement.

This system guarantees that every learner is assessed fairly, while also making the process flexible enough to suit working professionals and students alike.
