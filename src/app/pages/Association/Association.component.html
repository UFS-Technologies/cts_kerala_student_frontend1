Associate with C-TECH

The Council for Technology & Science Kerala (C-TECH), under Kairali Sevak Samaj, is dedicated to promoting skill development, vocational training, and career-oriented education across Kerala.

We invite educational institutions, training centers, and academies to become our Institutional Members and collaborate with us in shaping a skilled generation for the future.

Importance of Institutional Membership

Be part of a recognized Skill Development Council working in association with national-level sector skill bodies. Gain the trust and credibility of offering career-relevant training programs under C-TECH. Opportunity to conduct approved skill-based diplomas, certificate courses, online training & examinations. Access to syllabus, evaluation, certification & digital student record system managed by C-TECH. Priority in Government & CSR skill projects implemented through C-TECH.

Opportunities for Institutions

Launch new vocational and professional courses under C-TECH guidance. Associate with Kerala-wide training networks for student exchange and collaborations. Increase student admissions through recognized certifications & career opportunities abroad. Get access to faculty development programs, workshops & seminars conducted by CTECH. Joint participation in skill exhibitions, career fairs & job placement drives.

Membership Fee

Institutional Membership Fee: ₹ 5,000

Income & Opportunities for Institutions with C-TECH

Institutions can collect course/training fees from students directly as per their infrastructure and faculty availability.

C-TECH provides industry-recognized course certificates, ensuring better career prospects for students in India and abroad.

By associating with C-TECH, institutions get the opportunity to run UGC-recognized university certificate/diploma programs.

Institutions can become part of Kairali Sevak Samaj’s Continuing Education initiatives, opening doors to wider student networks.

Through C-TECH, students can enjoy credit transfer opportunities for completing degree programs faster and with flexibility.

Partner institutions get access to new educational ideas, updated skill programs, and modern curriculum in sync with industry demand.

Institutions need to contribute only ₹1000 per student as service charge to C-TECH for registration, exam, and certification support.

Some of C-TECH’s skill programs are PSC approved, and this recognition can be highlighted in institutional publicity and promotions.

C-TECH provides career counseling, employability training, and placement guidance to students enrolled under member institutions.

Association with C-TECH opens access to NSDC, Sector Skill Councils, MSME, and international skill certification pathways.

Institutional members can use C-TECH brand support, ISO-certified systems, and publicity networks to enhance their reputation.

Access to short-term, job-oriented, and fast-track diplomas that attract more admissions and increase institutional revenue.
