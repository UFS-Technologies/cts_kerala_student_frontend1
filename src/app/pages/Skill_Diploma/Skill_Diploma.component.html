Certificate & Diploma Programs

 

Council for Technology & Science Kerala (C-TECH), operating under Kairali Sevak Samaj, conducts a wide range of Diploma and Skill Training Programs across Kerala. These programs are delivered in collaboration with multiple authorized training centres that are associated with C-TECH and follow our curriculum, quality standards, and assessment protocols.

Our goal is to make skill-based education affordable and accessible to everyone—especially youth, working professionals, and those with informal experience but no formal qualification. All programs focus on practical skills, job readiness, and career advancement.

Program Categories & Career Opportunities

Engineering Technology & Applied Science Programs: Practical diploma training in mechanical, civil, electrical, and allied trades.

Jobs: Site Supervisors, Maintenance Technicians, Junior Engineers.

Computing & Information Technology Programs: Covers software, hardware, networking, and computer basics.

Jobs: IT Support Staff, Programmers, Network Technicians.

Business & Management Programs: Includes business skills, entrepreneurship, and operations.

Jobs: Office Executives, Startup Owners, Project Coordinators.

Paramedical Science Programs: Hands-on medical support training including lab, X-ray, and EMT.

Jobs: Lab Technicians, Paramedics, Diagnostic Assistants.

Yoga Science Programs: Courses in traditional and therapeutic yoga practices.

Jobs: Yoga Trainers, Wellness Instructors, Health Coaches.

Journalism & Mass Communication Programs: Training in media writing, editing, and video production.

Jobs: Journalists, Content Creators, Media Assistants.

Hospitality & Tourism Management Programs : Hotel operations, front office, food service, and travel.

Jobs: Hotel Staff, Tour Operators, Receptionists.

Music & Dance Programs: Focus on Indian and Western music, classical and modern dance.

Jobs: Instructors, Stage Performers, Recording Artists.

Solar Energy Programs: Installation and maintenance of solar systems.

Jobs: Solar Technicians, Field Installers.

Safety Engineering Programs: Training in industrial safety, fire safety, and hazard control.

Jobs: Safety Officers, Site Safety Managers.

Aviation Education Programs: Includes airport operations, ground handling, and cabin crew training.

Jobs: Ground Staff, Ticketing Agents, Cabin Crew Assistants.

Shipping Education Programs: Covers logistics, maritime trade, and port services.

Jobs: Logistics Coordinators, Port Staff.

Interior & Exterior Design Programs: Design techniques, software tools, and site planning.

Jobs: Interior Designers, CAD Draftsmen.

Beauty Education Programs: Training in cosmetology, skincare, hair styling.

Jobs: Beauticians, Salon Professionals.

Textile Education Programs: Pattern making, fashion design, stitching & garment handling.

Jobs: Tailors, Boutique Owners, Designers.

Food & Beverage Education Programs: Cooking skills, food hygiene, catering & hospitality.

Jobs: Chefs, Catering Staff, Food Handlers.

Cell Phone Technology Programs: Mobile repair, software flashing, hardware diagnostics.

Jobs: Mobile Technicians, Service Centre Operators.

Lift Technology Education Programs: Installation and maintenance of elevators.

Jobs: Lift Mechanics, Maintenance Staff.

Dental Ceramic Technology Programs: Fabrication of dental prosthetics and lab operations.

Jobs: Dental Technicians, Lab Assistants.

CCTV Technology Education Programs : Installation and configuration of security systems.

Jobs: CCTV Installers, Freelance Technicians.

Pre-Primary & Montessori Training Programs: Child psychology, activity-based education methods.

Jobs: Nursery Teachers, Montessori Instructors.

Health & Fitness Education Programs:Training in exercise science, gym equipment, nutrition.

Jobs: Personal Trainers, Gym Instructors.

Ayurvedic & Panchakarma Education Programs: Ayurvedic healing and wellness therapy training.

Jobs: Spa Therapists, Panchakarma Assistants.

Astrology & Tantrik Education Programs: Courses in Vedic astrology, rituals, and predictions.

Jobs: Astrologers, Spiritual Advisors.

Hospital Administration, Nursing & Health Programs: Hospital office management, basic patient care.

Jobs: Nursing Assistants, Hospital Clerks.

Business Administration Training Programs: HR, accounts, communication, organizational skills.

Jobs: Admin Officers, HR Assistants.

Film Technology & Photography Programs: Camera work, editing, script writing, digital content.

Jobs: Photographers, Videographers, Editors.

Office Secretaryship Training Programs: Typing, documentation, front office operations.

Jobs: Secretaries, Office Assistants.

Office Management Education Programs: Coordination, file management, staff handling.

Jobs: Office Managers, Executive Assistants.
