University Programs

University-Integrated Skill Certification Programs by C-TECH

In association with UGC-recognized universities like Scope Global Skills University, Council for Technology & Science Kerala (C-TECH) offers a wide range of industry-relevant certificate and diploma programs across Kerala. These programs are delivered through C-TECH affiliated training centres and come with university-approved certificates—valuable for both domestic employment and overseas job opportunities, including in GCC, Europe, and Southeast Asia.

This initiative ensures academic credibility, skill recognition, and employability, especially for students, job seekers, and working professionals looking for skill-based advancement or migration benefits.
