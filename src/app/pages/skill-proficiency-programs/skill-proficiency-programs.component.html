Skill Proficiency Programs

 

The Skill Proficiency Program by C-TECH is designed to certify practical skills in a wide range of trades and technical areas. These programs are especially beneficial for those who already have experience or informal training in a particular skill, but lack formal certification. Through a structured assessment and certification process, C-TECH validates your abilities and awards you a recognized Skill Proficiency Certificate.

Whether you're a student, job seeker, self-employed technician, or working professional—this program enhances your credibility, employability, and migration readiness.

Program Objectives:

Identify and validate existing technical skills

Help skilled individuals gain formal recognition

Enhance employment opportunities in India and abroad

Support career growth, promotions, and eligibility for government/private job roles

Strengthen portfolio for migration or licensing abroad

Who Can Apply?

Candidates with practical experience in a specific skill/trade

ITI / Diploma holders looking for upskilling or recognition

Professionals working in unorganized sectors without certificates

Students of skill development centers

Returnees from abroad who want certification for local use

Job seekers aiming for Gulf, Europe, or international employment

Trades Covered (examples):

Electrician / Wiring Technician, Welding (ARC, TIG, MIG), Plumbing, Mobile Phone Servicing, CCTV Installation, Computer Hardware & Networking, Tailoring & Sewing Machine Operation, Beautician / Hair Stylist, Car Mechanic / Auto Electrician, AC & Refrigerator Technician, Housekeeping / Hospitality Skills, Yoga / Ayurveda Assistant, Data Entry / Office Automation (And many more as per local demand and centre capability)

Certification Process:

Skill Assessment Test – Conducted at C-TECH affiliated centers

Practical Demonstration or Viva

Verification of Experience (if applicable)

Issuance of C-TECH Skill Proficiency Certificate

Benefits for Students & Candidates:

Adds value to your resume and career profile

Recognized skill certificate useful for job applications

Helpful for GCC attestation and migration interviews

Improves chances of placements through training partners

Boosts confidence and credibility in your profession

Global Relevance:

Many international employers, especially in GCC and Southeast Asia, look for verified skill certificates

Skill Proficiency Certificates are considered proof of practical ability, especially in blue-collar job sectors

C-TECH certification can support candidates during embassy verification or attestation processes

Program Delivery:

Offered through C-TECH affiliated Skill Test Centers across Kerala. Sessions available monthly or on-demand, based on trade availability.
