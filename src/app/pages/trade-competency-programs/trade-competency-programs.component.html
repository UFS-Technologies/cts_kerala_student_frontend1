Trade Competency Programs

About the Program

The Trade Competency Program is a certification initiative conducted by Council for Technology & Science Kerala (C-TECH) to formally recognize the skills and practical knowledge of individuals working in various trades and technical fields. These programs are especially beneficial for those who have learned their craft through experience but lack formal education or certification.

C-TECH, in collaboration with experienced professionals, training centers, and skill assessment experts, identifies eligible candidates and certifies them after a thorough evaluation of their competency.

Importance of Trade Competency Certification

Recognition of Informal Learning: Many individuals acquire technical skills through hands-on experience or apprenticeship. This program offers official recognition for such expertise.

Boost Employability: Certified individuals are more likely to be hired by employers, especially in technical trades and government contracts where certified manpower is preferred.

Career Progression: Certification helps workers move to higher job roles or supervisory positions.

Suitable for Migration & Contract Jobs: Trade certificates are highly valued in Gulf countries and abroad, helping skilled workers qualify for technical positions overseas.

Confidence & Identity: The certificate acts as a mark of confidence and pride for individuals who have developed skills without formal education.

Trades Covered (Indicative List)

Electrician, Plumber, Welder, Mason, Carpenter, Auto Mechanic, AC/Refrigeration Technician, Mobile Phone Repair Technician, Tailor/Dressmaker, Solar Panel Installer, CCTV Technician, Lift Technician, House Wiring Technician, Painter, Beautician, Assistant Cook And more...

C-TECH’s Role in the Program

Develops trade-wise certification standards and assessment rubrics.

Collaborates with recognized training centers and trade professionals for candidate evaluation.

Issues government-recognized skill certification after proper verification.

Maintains a central registry of certified individuals for employer verification.

Conducts awareness campaigns and field visits to promote skill recognition among informal sector workers.

Eligibility Criteria

Minimum age: 18 years

Basic literacy preferred (not mandatory)

Minimum 1–2 years’ practical experience in the relevant trade

Proof of work experience (photos, tools used, employer reference,] or self-employed documentation)

Admission & Certification Procedure

Application: Submit a Trade Competency Certification Application Form at a nearby C-TECH affiliated center.

Document Collection: Provide supporting documents such as ID proof, experience evidence, or tools used.

BAssessment: Appear for a practical skill test or on-site evaluation by expert assessors.

Verification: C-TECH verifies the authenticity of the skill through physical/online interviews or trade tests.

Certification: Successful candidates receive a C-TECH Trade Competency Certificate with trade name, skill level, and photo ID.

Student Benefits

Government-recognized certificate

Increased job opportunities locally and abroad

Identity as a certified professional

Access to further advanced skill development programs

Recognition from local employers, contractors, and government bodies

Who Should Apply?

Informal workers in unorganized sectors

Workers seeking recognition for foreign employment

Skilled individuals without formal schooling

Women workers and home-based earners

Small business workers (e.g., electricians, tailors, drivers, etc.)

How to Enroll?

Contact your nearest C-TECH Authorized Skill Training Center or apply through the official C-TECH website. Field officers and training center coordinators are available to assist you throughout the process.
